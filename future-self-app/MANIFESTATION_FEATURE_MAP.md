# Manifestation Feature Map

## Product Thesis

Future Self should not compete as a plain AI image app.

It should compete as a manifestation, self-improvement, and goal-achievement app that uses AI images to make the user's desired future feel tangible.

The images are the hook.
The daily practice is the habit.
The progress system is the retention.

## What Existing Apps Tend To Do

Successful apps in this space usually combine several of these patterns:

- Daily affirmations
- Vision boards
- Gratitude journals
- Scripting prompts
- Goal setting
- Habit streaks
- Mood check-ins
- Notifications
- Audio or guided visualization
- Lockscreen / widget reminders
- Premium content packs
- Subscription unlocks

Future Self should include the strongest versions of these patterns, then add personalized future-self imagery on top.

## Core Feature Pillars

### 1. Vision Builder

Purpose:
Help the user define what they actually want.

Features:
- Goal category picker
- Guided interview
- Desired identity statement
- Environment and lifestyle details
- Emotion selector
- Timeline selector
- Obstacles and limiting beliefs
- Personal "why"

Output:
- Future self summary
- Manifestation statement
- AI prompt seed
- Daily practice plan

### 2. Future Self Image Studio

Purpose:
Make the desired identity feel visible and personal.

Features:
- Selfie upload
- Consent flow
- Scene selection
- Style selection
- Image generation
- Regenerate variations
- Favorite images
- Save to vision board
- Export to photos
- Share to social
- Lock screen export

Scene examples:
- Book signing
- Dream office
- Fitness transformation
- Family home
- Wedding / relationship
- Founder keynote
- Luxury travel
- Peaceful morning routine
- Graduation
- New apartment

### 3. Vision Board

Purpose:
Give the user a daily visual anchor.

Features:
- Boards by goal
- AI-generated images
- Uploaded inspiration images
- Affirmation cards
- Goal milestones
- Progress images
- Drag-to-reorder layout
- Daily featured image

### 4. Daily Manifestation Home

Purpose:
Turn inspiration into a repeatable habit.

Daily modules:
- Today's future-self image
- Personalized affirmation
- Scripting prompt
- Gratitude prompt
- One aligned action
- Belief check-in
- Streak progress

Example:
"Today, act like the author version of yourself. Write 300 words and script the moment your first reader messages you."

### 5. Scripting Journal

Purpose:
Help the user write from the identity of the person they are becoming.

Features:
- Daily prompt
- Category-specific prompt packs
- Voice-to-text
- Past entries
- Highlight emotional phrases
- Convert journal entry into affirmation
- Convert journal entry into image prompt

Prompt examples:
- "Write about waking up in the home you manifested."
- "Describe the email confirming your dream job."
- "Describe the first person who tells you your book changed them."
- "Write as if your body already feels strong, healthy, and yours."

### 6. Affirmations

Purpose:
Give the user repeated identity reinforcement.

Features:
- Personalized affirmations
- Category-specific affirmations
- Morning and night modes
- Audio playback
- Lock screen cards
- Favorite affirmations
- Affirmation streaks

Affirmation examples:
- "I am becoming the kind of person who finishes what they start."
- "My future body is built through today's choices."
- "My work is valuable, visible, and needed."
- "I am available for a peaceful, loving family life."

### 7. Aligned Action Tracker

Purpose:
Keep manifestation grounded in behavior.

Features:
- One daily action
- Small wins log
- Weekly goal
- Habit streak
- Obstacle reflection
- Progress review

Action examples:
- Apply to one job.
- Write 300 words.
- Walk for 30 minutes.
- Message one potential customer.
- Save $25.
- Plan one family activity.

### 8. Belief And Mood Tracking

Purpose:
Measure the user's emotional relationship with their goal.

Features:
- Belief score from 1-10
- Mood check-in
- Confidence trend
- Resistance notes
- Weekly reflection

This gives the app a reason to adapt the daily prompts.

### 9. Weekly Future Drop

Purpose:
Create anticipation and subscription value.

Features:
- Weekly generated image pack
- Weekly affirmation set
- Weekly scripting theme
- Progress recap
- New scenes unlocked

Example:
"This week: see yourself receiving the keys, signing the contract, and celebrating the result."

## MVP Scope

Build only enough to prove the full loop:

- Choose one of three goals
- Answer guided questions
- Upload selfie placeholder
- See future self summary
- View generated placeholder image gallery
- Save to vision board
- Complete today's affirmation
- Complete scripting prompt
- Log one aligned action
- Track streak

First three goals:
- Wealth and lifestyle
- Perfect body
- Published author

## What Makes It Better

The app should feel better than existing manifestation apps because:

- It is more personal.
- It asks better questions.
- It turns vague wishes into concrete scenes.
- It connects visualization with action.
- It generates images that include the user's own likeness.
- It makes the future feel emotionally close.

## Important Product Guardrail

Do not promise guaranteed outcomes.

The app helps users visualize, focus, reflect, and act. It should not claim that images or affirmations directly cause wealth, beauty, health, relationships, pregnancy, career outcomes, or other life events.
