# Future Self App Blueprint

## Working Name

Future Self

Other possible names:
- Manifest
- Becoming
- Future You
- VisionSelf
- LifeFrame

## Core Idea

Future Self is a manifestation and transformation app that turns a user's goals into emotionally vivid AI-generated images of their future life.

The user uploads selfies, answers guided questions about who they want to become, and receives personalized visual outputs: themselves in their dream office, with their future body, in a richer lifestyle, leading a team, traveling, speaking on stage, owning assets, or living a specific aspirational identity.

The app is not just an image generator. It is a manifestation system with a visual identity simulator built into it.

## Positioning

Most manifestation apps help users write affirmations, build vision boards, or listen to audio.

Future Self makes the desired identity feel real by showing the user visual evidence of a possible future version of themselves.

The emotional promise:

"See yourself there before you get there."

## Target User

Primary users:
- People interested in manifestation, self-improvement, wealth, fitness, beauty, business, spirituality, and personal transformation.
- Creators and entrepreneurs who want visual motivation.
- People who already use vision boards, affirmations, journaling, Pinterest, Midjourney, ChatGPT, or avatar apps.

Best early niche:
- Ambitious young adults who want a "rich future self" or "glow-up future self" experience.

## App Store Category

Likely categories:
- Lifestyle
- Health & Fitness
- Productivity

Lifestyle is probably the best first category because the app mixes identity, motivation, visualization, and aesthetics.

## Product Principle

Future Self should work at least as well as a traditional manifestation app before image generation is added.

The core loop is:

1. Clarify the desire.
2. Turn it into a specific identity and goal.
3. Visualize it daily.
4. Script it as if it is becoming real.
5. Take aligned actions.
6. Track emotional belief, consistency, and progress.
7. Refresh the vision with personalized images.

AI images are the emotional amplifier. The manifestation system is the retention engine.

## Manifestation Methods To Support

The app should combine the most common manifestation and self-improvement patterns:

- Vision boards
- Daily affirmations

- Gratitude journaling

The user should not feel like they are only creating pictures. They should feel like they are practicing becoming a future version of themselves.

## MVP Promise

In the first version, the app should do one thing extremely well:

Guide the user from a desire into a daily manifestation practice, then generate a personalized "future self" photo set that makes the desired identity feel real.

## MVP User Flow

1. Welcome
   - Short emotional intro.
   - User starts by choosing the future they want to visualize.

2. Choose Transformation Type
   - Wealth & luxury
   - Founder / CEO
   - Fitness transformation
   - Dream relationship
   - Published author / creator
   - Dream job
   - Family and home
   - Confidence / glow-up
   - Travel lifestyle
   - Spiritual / peaceful life
   - Custom vision

3. Upload Selfies
   - Ask for 3-8 clear selfies.
   - Explain that better photos create better results.
   - Require consent to generate AI images using their likeness.

4. Guided Vision Interview
   - What do you want to become?
   - Where are you living?
   - What do you do for work?
   - What does your office or environment look like?
   - How wealthy or successful do you want the image to feel?
   - What objects are part of your future life?
   - What is your fashion style?
   - Who is around you?
   - What emotion should the image capture?

5. Future Self Preview
   - App summarizes the chosen identity.
   - User can edit before generating.

6. Generate Photo Set
   - Produce 4-12 images.
   - Example outputs:
     - User in a luxury office.
     - User stepping onto a private jet.
     - User beside a yacht.
     - User leading a team meeting.
     - User in a high-end apartment.
     - User at a gala or launch event.

7. Save Vision Board
   - Generated images become a board.
   - User can favorite images, regenerate, download, or share.

8. Daily Manifestation Practice
   - Daily affirmation based on the user's vision.
   - Daily scripting prompt.
   - Gratitude entry.
   - One aligned action.
   - Belief check-in.
   - Daily reminder notification.
   - Optional lock screen style image export.

9. Progress and Renewal
   - Track streaks.
   - Track completed actions.
   - Regenerate new future-self scenes weekly.
   - Reflect on how the user's identity is changing.

## AI Generation Approach

The app needs two AI layers:

1. Vision-to-prompt layer
   - Converts the user's answers into high-quality image prompts.
   - Keeps the user's face, desired environment, clothing, emotion, and cinematic style consistent.

2. Image generation layer
   - Uses the user's uploaded selfies as likeness references.
   - Generates aspirational but clearly AI-created images.

Important: the app must handle likeness consent carefully. Users should only generate images of themselves or people they have explicit permission to use.

## Safety and App Store Notes

The app should avoid promising guaranteed life outcomes.

Good language:
- "Visualize your future self."
- "Create motivational images."
- "Build a personal vision board."
- "Explore who you are becoming."

Avoid language:
- "This will make you rich."
- "Guaranteed transformation."
- "Scientifically proven to manifest wealth."
- "Predict your actual future."

The app should include:
- Clear AI disclosure.
- Consent checkbox before selfie upload.
- Privacy policy explaining how photos are stored, used, and deleted.
- Delete-my-data option.
- No generation of misleading political, sexual, exploitative, or non-consensual likeness content.

## Technical Architecture

Recommended first build:

- Mobile app: React Native with Expo
- Backend: Node.js API
- Database: Supabase or PostgreSQL
- Auth: Apple Sign In, email magic link
- Storage: S3-compatible object storage or Supabase Storage
- Payments: Apple in-app purchases
- AI:
  - Text model for interview summary and prompt generation
  - Image model for future-self image generation

Why React Native / Expo:
- Faster MVP.
- Easier iteration.
- Can still ship to the Apple App Store.
- Good enough for a photo-generation consumer app.

## Category Benchmarking Rules

We should study successful manifestation, affirmation, journaling, habit, and goal apps closely, but we should not copy another product exactly.

What to borrow:
- Common onboarding structure
- Daily streak mechanics
- Affirmation delivery
- Vision board creation
- Scripting prompts
- Reminder timing
- Subscription gates
- Progress celebrations

What to make original:
- Visual design
- Copywriting
- Question flow
- AI image scenes
- Brand voice
- Iconography
- Layouts
- Naming
- Manifestation framework

The goal is to be familiar enough that users understand it instantly, but differentiated enough that the app feels new.

## Daily Product Loop

The home screen should answer one question:

"What do I do today to become this future self?"

Daily loop:

1. View today's future-self image.
2. Read or listen to a personalized affirmation.
3. Complete a 2-minute scripting prompt.
4. Log one aligned action.
5. Check belief level from 1-10.
6. Save a short reflection.
7. Earn streak progress.

Weekly loop:

1. Review progress.
2. Update the goal.
3. Generate a new image pack.
4. Add the best image to the vision board.
5. Choose next week's identity focus.
